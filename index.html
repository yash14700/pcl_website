Company Profile

 

Parkinson Clarke was incorporated in 1983 under the laws of Hong Kong. The initial objective of the company was to source machinery and materials for a select set of projects to business groups.

 

We have sourced and supplied machinery and raw materials for several projects like textile factories, paper and stationery plants, crushing and packing plants, and garment units. We continue to be involved with serving new projects.

 

Over the years, PCL has diversified into sourcing general merchandise and supplying to customers, mainly based in Africa and other Continents. We cover a wide range of General Merchandise like Textiles, Clothing and Accessories, Cosmetics, Batteries, Paper and Stationery products, Household products, Travel goods, Electrical and Electronic appliances, etc and also Food Commodities like Tomato paste, Sugar, Rice, Cooking oil, Wheat flour, Milk powder etc. With such a diversification, we have achieved an accumulated turnover of USD 500 Million

 

PCL has poised itself to source at most economical prices, a wide range of articles, directly from manufacturers and arrange shipments promptly and efficiently. Customer satisfaction is evident from the continuous repeat orders.

 

The company has big in-transit warehouse in DUBAI, from that it can mix merchandise in containers for customers who may need such services. However, we mainly do direct shipments from factories to destination ports.

 

The company has efficient and experienced merchandising and shipping staff, good reputation with its bankers and strong support from its promoters.
 

Our strengths lie in:

  • Supplier relationships
  • Logistics
  • Customer Support and Services
