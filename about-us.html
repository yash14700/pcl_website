About Us

PCLME was incorporated in 1983 under the laws of Hong Kong and started operations as a trading company. Since inception, the company has grown rapidly into a multi-location global organization with investments in manufacturing, trading and distribution.

Our Vission

To be the leading player in commodity trading industry

Our Mission

Develop long term mutually beneficial relationships with suppliers and customers sustained with economic value addition. Provide customers with world-class solutions. Creation of Export Home Markets

Quality Policy

Parkinson Clarke is dedicated to achieve and maintain total customer satisfaction by supplying only the highest quality products and services while maintaining high level of ethical standards.

Parkinson Clarke is committed to ensure timely delivery of supply of general materials and equipment to meet customer's requirements. We ensure this through the effective implementation and continual improvement of quality management systems and through teamwork.

We offer continuous improvement of worker's efficiency by developing their technical skill through periodical training.

We develop strategic plans and objectives for business development as a continual improvement.

We provide services in compliance with applicable legal and regulatory requirements.

Parkinson Clarke (Middle East)

Parkinson Clarke Middle East is headquartered in Dubai, UAE. PCLME has already established itself as one of the main suppliers in the paper and textile industry and now aims to be a leader in commodity specific industries. PCLME has worked with governments, NGOs and the UN to cater to their required supplies in Africa.
